      What are the most essential technologies that made the World Wide Web such a success?

      - The essential technologies for the World Wide Web are Uniform Resource Identifier (URI) and HyperText Transfer Protocol (HTTP).

      How are resources (html pages, images, etc) addressed in the web?

      - They are addressed with an URL.

      What are the general components of a url?

      - An URL indicates a protocol (http), a hostname (www.example.com) and a file name(index.html).
